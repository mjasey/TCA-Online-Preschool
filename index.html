Our Foundation

A classroom built on God's Word.

At Taylor Christian Academy, scripture isn't a subject — it's the ground we stand on. Every lesson, song, and story points back to Jesus.

We keep our classes small so every child is known by name, encouraged in their unique gifts, and given the time to wonder, ask questions, and grow.
