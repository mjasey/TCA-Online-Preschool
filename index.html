Curriculum

What Your Child Will Learn

Our comprehensive preschool curriculum combines academics and faith-based learning to build a strong foundation in every area of your child's development.

01

Literacy & Reading

Building strong foundations in phonics, letter recognition, and early reading skills through fun interactive activities.

02

Math & Numbers

Introducing numbers, counting, patterns and basic math concepts through hands-on learning and movement activities.

03

Science Exploration

Nurturing natural curiosity through simple science experiments and discovery activities that make learning exciting.

04

Bible & Faith

Scripture, values and character woven throughout every lesson. Children learn God's Word through stories, songs and prayer.

05

Arts & Creativity

Music, movement, drawing and creative expression develop fine motor skills and nurture each child's God-given talents.

06

Public Speaking & Confidence

Building confidence in speaking and expressing ideas through show and tell, group discussions and presentations.

Sherri Taylor
Founder & CEO

Sherri Taylor founded Taylor Christian Academy of the Arts with a singular vision: to make exceptional, faith-rooted education accessible to every family. She believes all children deserve a quality education in a loving, safe environment — and should have fun while attaining it. As a Christian parent, she ensured her own daughter — a recent Harvard graduate and now a Law Clerk at the United States Court of Appeals — enjoyed a quality education that prepared her for lasting success. This is exactly what TCA will make happen for children all over the world.
