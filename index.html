Our Approach

Four Lenses on Every Lesson.

Each class is designed around four intertwined ideas — what children build, what they study, how we teach, and how a day feels.

i.

Skills We Build

Confidence with letters, numbers, scripture, and self-expression — the foundation of every future classroom.

Letter Recognition Number Confidence Bible Knowledge Public Speaking Creative Expression Social Skills
ii.

Subjects We Cover

A rigorous preschool curriculum that moves between core academics and the arts in every single session.

Literacy Math Science Arts
iii.

How We Teach

Small classes, patient repetition, and hands-on learning — shaped by the conviction that how we teach matters as much as what we teach.

Repetition Hands-On Meaningful Relationships Whole Child
iv.

A Day in Class

Every class follows a gentle rhythm — we begin in prayer, move through learning, make something with our hands, and close with encouragement.

Faith First Academic Creative Confidence
