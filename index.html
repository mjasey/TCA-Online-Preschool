Founder & CEO
Sherri Taylor

Sherri Taylor founded Taylor Christian Academy of the Arts with a singular vision: to make exceptional, faith-rooted education accessible to every family. She believes all children deserve a quality education in a loving, safe environment — and should have fun while attaining it. As a Christian parent, she ensured her own daughter — a recent Harvard graduate and now a Law Clerk at the United States Court of Appeals — enjoyed a quality education that prepared her for lasting success. This is exactly what TCA will make happen for children all over the world.
