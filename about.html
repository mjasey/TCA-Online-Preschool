Welcome

Welcome to TCA

Preschool is crucial for building a strong foundation during a critical period of brain development. It fosters language, social, cognitive, and emotional skills, setting the stage for future success for your child.

We offer a comprehensive preschool curriculum with a great blend of academics and faith-based learning. Our program is designed to build a strong foundation in literacy, math, science and critical thinking skills while nurturing creativity and a love for learning. With a rigorous, age-appropriate approach, students are challenged to grow intellectually and spiritually, preparing them for success in kindergarten and beyond.

Why Christian Education

The Benefits of a Christ-Centered School

There are many benefits to attending a Christian school. One of which is that your child will gain a strong Christian foundation. With prayer embedded in the school day and the curriculum aligning with biblical principles, each student will thrive in an environment that nurtures their growth. Their character will be developed in a positive way because of the physically and emotionally safe atmosphere. Additionally, there is a strong sense of family and unity.

Gaining a strong Christian foundation is crucial for students. Students will not only learn about God and His love, but they will see the love of God in action daily through their teachers, who will become great role models for them. In turn, students will seek a relationship with God and learn to make moral and ethical choices.

Prayer is a key component to the school day. Not only is it the path to a personal relationship with God, but it also strengthens students' relationships with one another. Core Competencies

What Every TCA Student Will Develop

Our Core Competencies provide a strong foundation for TCA and the families we serve.

Self-Awareness

Students know who they are in Christ Jesus — their values, beliefs, passion, gifts and talents expressed with confidence.

Communication

Students articulate and exchange ideas effectively, presenting themselves competently in any setting.

Leadership

Students lead with confidence, take on responsibility, solve problems and work effectively as a team.

Creativity

Students produce new ideas through daily journaling, brainstorming and STEAM projects — trained to think innovatively.

Critical Thinking

Students make well-reasoned conclusions, questioning and thinking independently rather than accepting everything.

Collaboration & Unity

Students work together as a team, sharing ideas and welcoming contributions and knowledge from peers.

Motivation

Students are active participants in their learning, eager to collaborate and apply what they have learned.

Personal Growth & Well-Being

Students understand the importance of self-care — emotionally, intellectually, physically, socially and spiritually.
